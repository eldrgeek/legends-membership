Charter & Goals

Subcommittee Goals

The Legends Life subcommittee is dedicated to ensuring that no former professional basketball player navigates the challenges of post-career life alone. We connect members with the resources, networks, and support they need to thrive — at every stage of life beyond the game.

Legends Life Platform — Initial Rollout Steps

The Legends Life rollout follows a phased approach built around three tiers: Legends Assist (the trusted front door), Legends Growth (career, health, and financial support), and Legends Legacy (leadership, mentorship, and community). Launch begins with Legends Assist — establishing one trusted point of entry for every member.

Phase 1 — Foundation
Form the Legends Life Steering Committee and establish the Legends Assist intake function as the platform's front door
Phase 2 — Engage
Conduct member needs assessments, build the partner/resource network, and begin connecting members to health, career, and financial services
Phase 3 — Grow
Launch Legends Growth programming, activate the Business Leaders Council, and establish mentorship matching across chapters
Phase 4 — Legacy
Activate Legends Legacy programming — community leadership, Brotherhood initiatives, Legends Speakers Bureau, and World Basketball Day
  • 1
    Launch Legends Assist — One Trusted Point of Entry

    Establish Legends Assist as the front door to the entire Legends Life platform. Every member who has a question, challenge, opportunity, or need will know exactly where to start. Legends Assist coordinates referrals to NBRPA programs, local chapters, healthcare providers, career resources, financial education partners, and the broader trusted network — providing a personalized, warm handoff rather than a phone directory.

  • 2
    Career Transition Resources — Second-Career Planning & Reinvention

    Provide comprehensive career transition resources for former professional basketball players, covering second-career planning, entrepreneurship, coaching, broadcasting, public speaking, and beyond. Connect members with career development partners, executive networking opportunities, the Business Leaders Council, and educational pathways through HBCU and university partners.

  • 3
    Financial Planning, Health & Wellness Support

    Connect members with vetted financial planning professionals, wealth preservation resources, healthcare partners, and wellness programs tailored to the unique needs of former professional athletes. Health programming encompasses heart health, brain health, orthopedic wellness, mental health, healthy aging, and chronic pain management — addressing the real health challenges members face after years of elite competition.

  • 4
    Peer Mentorship — Legends Mentor Initiative

    Build peer mentorship networks among retired players, pairing Legends who have navigated successful transitions with members who are earlier in their post-career journey. Extend mentorship outward to younger retired players, HBCU basketball players, and youth in local communities — activating the Brotherhood to create genuine lasting impact.

  • 5
    Member Needs Assessments

    Conduct Member Needs Assessments to understand each member's individual support requirements — career stage, health priorities, financial situation, family needs — and match them with the most relevant programs and resources. Take the assessment →

  • 6
    Coordination with NBA, NBPA & Alumni Associations

    Coordinate with the NBA, NBPA, and alumni associations to build a seamless player lifecycle — where active players enter retirement already connected to the Legends Life platform through Legends Launch (the NBA–NBPA–NBRPA Transition Pathway). Avoid duplication, expand available resources, and create warm introductions between organizations.

  • 7
    Life Insurance, Estate Planning & Retirement Benefits

    Help members navigate life insurance options, estate planning resources, and retirement benefit programs available to former professional basketball players — ensuring every Legend is financially protected and prepared for every stage of life beyond the game.

  • 8
    Legends Legacy — Brotherhood, Community & Lasting Impact

    Activate the Legends Legacy tier of the platform — community leadership, youth development, hospital visits, senior member support, the Legends Speakers Bureau, and NBRPA World Basketball Day. Ensure every Legend has the opportunity to give back, stay connected, and build a lasting legacy beyond their playing career.

About This Sub-Committee

Legends Life

The Transition Services sub-committee — operating under the Legends Life banner — is dedicated to helping former professional basketball players navigate life after their playing careers.

Professional basketball careers are extraordinary journeys, but every career eventually comes to an end. The transition to life after the game can be one of the most challenging passages a player faces — financially, socially, and personally. The Legends Life initiative exists to ensure no former player walks that road alone.

Through this sub-committee, the Membership Services Committee coordinates resources, connects members with relevant programs, and advocates for the long-term wellbeing of the Legends of Basketball community.

Charter & Goals

Subcommittee Goals

The Legends Life subcommittee is dedicated to ensuring that no former professional basketball player navigates the challenges of post-career life alone. We connect members with the resources, networks, and support they need to thrive.

  • 1
    Career Transition Resources

    Provide comprehensive career transition resources for former professional basketball players, covering second-career planning, entrepreneurship, coaching, broadcasting, and beyond.

  • 2
    Financial Planning, Health & Wellness Support

    Connect members with vetted financial planning professionals, health care resources, and wellness programs tailored to the unique needs of former professional athletes.

  • 3
    Peer Mentorship Networks

    Build peer mentorship networks among retired players, pairing those who have navigated successful transitions with members who are earlier in their post-career journey.

  • 4
    Member Needs Assessments

    Conduct Member Needs Assessments to identify each member's individual support requirements and match them with the most relevant programs and resources. Take the assessment →

  • 5
    Coordination with Alumni Associations

    Coordinate with NBA, ABA, and WNBA alumni associations to expand the resources available to Legends members and avoid duplication of effort.

  • 6
    Life Insurance, Estate Planning & Retirement Benefits

    Help members navigate life insurance options, estate planning resources, and retirement benefit programs available to former professional basketball players.

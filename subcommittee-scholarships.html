About Scholarship America

Founded in 1958, Scholarship America is the nation's most trusted scholarship management organization. For over 65 years they have helped corporations, foundations, and nonprofits like the Legends of Basketball turn educational giving into measurable impact — handling everything from program design through award disbursement so the sponsoring organization can focus on strategy, not administration.

What They Handle for Legends

  • Program design — criteria development, eligibility rules, and award structure tailored to Legends' mission
  • Application management — custom-branded application portal, processing, and applicant communications
  • Selection support — screening, scoring, and finalist review coordination
  • Award disbursement — direct payment to students' institutions, ensuring funds reach recipients correctly
  • Compliance & reporting — IRS compliance, audit trails, and annual program impact reports
  • Student progress tracking — ongoing contact with recipients to track academic outcomes

Learn more at scholarshipamerica.org

Eligibility & Requirements

Scholarship Eligibility

Each scholarship and grant program has its own eligibility requirements. The following guidelines reflect the general standards applied across Legends of Basketball scholarship programs. For program-specific details, contact the Scholarships & Grants subcommittee leads.

🎓
Academic Standing
  • Minimum cumulative GPA of 2.5 on a 4.0 scale (some programs require 3.0+)
  • Must be enrolled full-time or half-time at an accredited institution
  • Satisfactory Academic Progress (SAP) required to maintain and renew awards
  • GPA verified through official transcript at time of application and annually for renewals
🔄
Award Renewability
  • Most scholarships are renewable for up to 4 years of undergraduate study
  • Graduate-level awards are typically one-year, renewable based on continued eligibility
  • Recipients must reapply or submit a renewal form each academic year
  • Award amounts may be adjusted based on available funding each cycle
👤
Who Is Eligible
  • Current Legends of Basketball members in good standing
  • Dependent children of Legends members (age/dependency rules vary by program)
  • Spouses or domestic partners of Legends members (select programs)
  • HBCU Scholarship Program open to students attending accredited HBCUs regardless of family connection
📋
Application Requirements
  • Completed application form (submitted through Scholarship America portal)
  • Official transcripts from most recent academic institution
  • Personal statement or essay (topic varies by scholarship)
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Proof of enrollment or acceptance at an accredited institution
  • Verification of Legends member status or family relationship
⏰
Deadlines & Timing
  • Application windows typically open January – March for the following academic year
  • Award decisions are communicated by June/July for fall enrollment
  • Funds are disbursed directly to the recipient's institution each semester
  • Contact the subcommittee to get on the notification list for application openings
⚠️
Important Notes
  • Scholarship awards may not be used for room and board at some institutions — verify with your school's financial aid office
  • Recipients must maintain enrollment and GPA standards throughout the award period
  • Awards are not transferable to other individuals
  • Visit legendsofbasketball.com for the complete and most current eligibility requirements for each program

Questions about eligibility? Contact subcommittee leads Lionel Hollins (Lihollins14@gmail.com) or George Tinsley (Gtinsley@pengeo.com) for program-specific eligibility details.

Charter & Goals

Subcommittee Goals

This subcommittee is committed to making educational opportunity a defining legacy of the Legends of Basketball organization — for members' families and for the broader communities our members have served.

  • 1
    Maintain a Complete Scholarship & Grant Listing

    Keep a complete, current list of all scholarships and grants available through Legends of Basketball published on this website, updated as new opportunities are added or existing programs change.

  • 2
    Partner with Scholarship America for Full Administration

    Leverage Scholarship America's end-to-end program administration for all scholarship operations — from application intake through award disbursement and compliance reporting.

  • 3
    Annual Award Targets — 10 in Year One, Growing 20% Annually

    Establish an initial goal of 10 scholarship awards in the first program year, with a 20% annual growth target thereafter. Funding targets will be set to support these award levels.

  • 4
    Track Applications, Awards & Student Progress

    Maintain records of all applications received, awards given, and ongoing student progress for every scholarship recipient in the program.

  • 5
    Promote Scholarship Opportunities to All Members & Families

    Actively promote scholarship opportunities to all Legends of Basketball members and their eligible family members through chapter communications, the website, and direct outreach.

  • 6
    Pursue External Grants — NBA Foundation & Beyond

    Actively pursue grants from the NBA Foundation and other external sources to grow the scholarship fund beyond member-supported levels.

  • 7
    Annual Impact Report to the Full Committee

    Report scholarship program impact annually to the full Membership Services Committee, including total awards given, funds disbursed, student outcomes, and program growth.

Charter & Goals

Subcommittee Goals

The Chapter President Communication subcommittee exists to ensure every Legends of Basketball chapter president is connected, informed, and supported — so our chapters can operate at their full potential.

  • 1
    Establish Regular Communication

    Build and maintain consistent lines of communication with all Legends of Basketball chapter presidents nationwide.

  • 2
    Monthly Check-Ins & Quarterly Reports

    Conduct monthly check-ins with chapter presidents and submit quarterly written reports to the full Membership Services Committee.

  • 3
    Master Chapter President Directory

    Maintain an up-to-date master directory of all chapter presidents with current contact information, accessible to committee leadership.

  • 4
    Coordinate Joint Chapter Initiatives

    Facilitate coordination across chapters for joint events, community drives, and outreach programs that benefit the entire Legends network.

  • 5
    Timely Updates to Chapter Presidents

    Provide chapter presidents with timely updates on committee decisions, available resources, and organizational priorities.

  • 6
    Shared Communication Protocol

    Develop a shared communication template and reporting protocol to ensure consistent, high-quality chapter reporting across all regions.

Welcome

Where The Story Continues

The Membership Services Committee of Legends of Basketball is dedicated to supporting and advocating for the needs of former professional basketball players. We connect our members with resources, opportunities, and community — honoring their legacy while ensuring their continued well-being.

🏀

Who We Serve

Former professional players from the NBA, WNBA, American Basketball Association (ABA), and Harlem Globetrotters — every generation, every era.

🤝

What We Do

We advocate for member benefits, coordinate programming across our five pillars, and ensure every legend has access to the support and recognition they deserve.

⭐

Our Commitment

Ten dedicated committee members — themselves legends — work together to serve their peers with the same dedication they brought to the game.

Our Foundation

Five Pillars of Service

Everything we do is grounded in these five commitments to our membership.

🤝

Camaraderie

Fostering lasting bonds between players who share the unique experience of professional basketball at the highest level.

💪

Health

Supporting the physical and mental well-being of our members long after the final buzzer sounds.

💰

Financial Stability

Connecting members with resources and guidance to ensure long-term financial security and prosperity.

🌍

Community

Empowering legends to give back to the communities that supported their journeys through the game.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family

Recognizing that behind every legend is a family, and supporting them as whole people beyond basketball.

Committee

Ten Legends. One Mission.

Our committee brings together ten former NBA, ABA, WNBA, and Harlem Globetrotter alumni — leaders who know firsthand what it means to have the support of a great organization.

Mission

Our Purpose

"We serve the men and women who defined professional basketball — ensuring that every legend has access to the community, resources, and recognition they earned on the court."

The Membership Services Committee of Legends of Basketball is a standing body of the National Basketball Retired Players Association (NBRPA). We are committed to advocacy, programming, and community — serving former professional players across every era and every league.

Who We Are

A ten-member committee of former professional basketball players — themselves legends — elected and appointed to serve the NBRPA membership. We represent the NBA, WNBA, ABA, and Harlem Globetrotters.

Who We Serve

Every former professional basketball player — from NBA champions to ABA trailblazers to Globetrotter entertainers — who has given their career to the sport deserves support, community, and connection in retirement.

What We Do

We advocate for member benefits, coordinate programming across our five pillars (Camaraderie, Health, Financial Stability, Community, Family), and ensure legends maintain meaningful connections to the game and to each other.

Our Foundation

Legends of Basketball

Legends of Basketball is the official home of the National Basketball Retired Players Association — the organization that has supported former professional basketball players since 1992. With chapters across the country and a membership spanning generations of professional basketball, Legends of Basketball exists to serve the whole person beyond the game.

The Membership Services Committee is one of the key bodies that ensures every member receives the attention, advocacy, and programming they deserve. Our tagline says it all: "Where The Story Continues."

Five Pillars

What We Stand For

🤝

Camaraderie

The bond between players who shared the court — across eras, teams, and leagues — is unique and enduring. We nurture it.

💪

Health

Physical and mental wellness resources for former players navigating life after the rigors of professional basketball.

💰

Financial Stability

Connecting members with financial literacy resources, advisors, and planning support for long-term security.

🌍

Community

Giving legends the platform to give back — using their influence, resources, and stories to uplift their communities.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family

Supporting the families of our members — because the sacrifices and triumphs of a basketball career are always shared.

About Choo Smith

Choo Smith is a former professional basketball player who brings his experience and commitment to service to the Legends of Basketball Membership Services Committee. As a committee contributor, Choo helps open each session and ensures the work of the committee begins with focus and purpose.

His dedication to the NBRPA and to the well-being of fellow former players reflects the spirit of the Membership Services Committee — legends serving legends, ensuring that every player who gave to the game continues to receive support, connection, and community in return.

Committee Role

Choo Smith serves on the Membership Services Committee alongside Chairman Greg Foster and nine fellow committee members. He participates in committee sessions and contributes to the committee's mission of advocating for member benefits and programming across the five pillars of Camaraderie, Health, Financial Stability, Community, and Family.
